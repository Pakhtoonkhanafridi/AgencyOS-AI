# Product Requirements Document

## Product

AgencyOS AI

## Value Proposition

Help service agencies manage clients, team workflows, billing, AI-assisted communication, and audit proofs from one SaaS workspace.

## Target Users

- Travel agencies
- Tax and documentation agencies
- Real estate service teams
- Small consulting teams
- Freelancers growing into agency operations

## MVP Scope

- Authentication and organizations
- Role-based access control
- Client and lead records
- Deal pipeline
- Task management
- AI client summary
- Invoice tracking
- Blockchain proof preview

## Success Metrics

- 1 public production deployment
- 5 beta users interviewed
- 10 public build updates
- 70 percent coverage on critical business logic
- 1 system design case study
