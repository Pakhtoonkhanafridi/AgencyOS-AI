# Product Requirements Document

## Product Name

AgencyOS AI

## One-Line Value Proposition

AgencyOS AI helps service agencies manage clients, operations, billing, and AI-assisted work in one secure SaaS workspace.

## Target Users

- Small travel agencies
- Tax and documentation service agencies
- Real estate teams
- Consulting and service businesses
- Freelancers growing into agency operations

## Business Problem

Many agencies lose time and revenue because client records, invoices, notes, files, and communication history live in separate tools. Managers lack visibility, agents repeat manual work, and owners cannot measure pipeline or service performance quickly.

## Product Goals

1. Centralize client and deal operations.
2. Reduce manual writing and review work through AI assistance.
3. Improve management visibility with dashboards and reports.
4. Create trustworthy audit trails for important actions.
5. Prove production-grade full-stack engineering through a public build.

## MVP Scope

- Authentication and organizations
- Role-based dashboard
- Clients, leads, deals, tasks, and notes
- AI client summary endpoint
- Pipeline and revenue dashboard
- Invoice tracking foundation
- Blockchain proof preview
- Deployment and CI

## Non-Goals For MVP

- Complex accounting
- Full document storage lifecycle
- Mainnet blockchain payments
- Native mobile app
- Advanced workflow automation marketplace

## Success Metrics

- 5 beta agencies interviewed
- 3 active beta users by Month 3
- 10 active users by Month 6
- 1 deployed production URL
- 70 percent coverage on critical business logic by Month 6
- 12 public build logs or articles by Month 12

## Recruiter-Facing Engineering Signals

- Clear product scope
- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture
- Secure API boundaries
- AI feature design with validation and evaluation
- Blockchain used only where it adds audit value
- CI, tests, docs, issue workflow, and deployment readiness
